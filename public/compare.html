Welcome to MIRAGE - Quick Tutorial
1
Select Models

Click the "Show Models" button and select 4 AI models to compare. You can hover over each model to see examples of their images.

2
Enter Your Prompt

Insert a descriptive prompt in the text field to guide the models in generating images.

3
Generate Images

Click the "Generate" button and wait while the models create their images.

4
Review Outputs

Compare the images created by different models and look for recurring patterns.
